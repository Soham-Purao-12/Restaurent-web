When you sit down at Uncle Sammy's Restaurent, you're dining with the Molinari family and over 45 years of restaurant experience.
A Scottsdale tradition for more than a quarter century, we're a little piece of Italy right in the heart of the Valley. Our casual neighborhood atmosphere, combined with creative, timeless dishes make us a favorite for business lunches, casual nights out and special occasions of every kind.
From exceptional veal cuts, to fresh pasta and seafood, to an award-winning wine list, our made-from-scratch menu and warm service bring out the best in classic Italian cuisine.
